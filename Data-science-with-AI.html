Data Science with AI

Become a Future-Ready Data Scientist with AI Expertise

Unlock the power of data, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Our Data Science with AI course is crafted to make you an expert in analyzing complex data, building predictive models, and creating intelligent systems.

You will work on real-world datasets and cutting-edge tools to solve business problems, making you a highly sought-after professional in today's data-driven world.

  • Master Python for Data Science (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib).
  • Build and train Machine Learning models for prediction and classification.
  • Dive into Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning with TensorFlow.
  • Gain expertise in Data Visualization tools like Power BI & Tableau.

What You Will Learn

Our curriculum is designed to make you a complete job-ready Data Scientist.

Data Science Foundations

Python for Data Science

Master the essential libraries for data analysis and manipulation, including Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib.

Statistical Analysis

Understand the core statistical concepts that form the backbone of data science and machine learning.

Data Wrangling & EDA

Learn to clean, process, and perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on complex, real-world datasets.

Data Visualization

Create insightful and compelling charts and dashboards using tools like Tableau and Power BI.

Machine Learning & AI

Supervised & Unsupervised Learning

Build and train predictive models using key algorithms like Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, and K-Means Clustering.

Advanced Machine Learning

Work with advanced techniques like Decision Trees, Random Forests, and Support Vector Machines (SVM).

Deep Learning with TensorFlow

Step into the world of Artificial Intelligence by learning to build and train neural networks for complex tasks.

Capstone Project

Apply all your skills to a comprehensive, real-world Data Science project from start to finish.
